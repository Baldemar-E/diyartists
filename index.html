R Stevie Moore

Having recorded over 400 self-released albums since 1968, R. Stevie Moore is considered by many to be the "Godfather of home-recording".
Moore's extensive discography of odd ball Pop-rock has influenced many of today's Lo-fi recording artists including Ariel Pink & Mac Demarco.
